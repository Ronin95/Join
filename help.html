Learning Management System Project

Kanban is a popular Lean workflow management method for defining, managing, and improving services that deliver knowledge work. It helps you visualize work, maximize efficiency, and improve continuously. Work is represented on Kanban boards, allowing you to optimize work delivery across multiple teams and handle even the most complex projects in a single environment.

How does it work?

To create a new task, select the tab Add Task and fill in all fields. Finally, decide who you want to share this task with and press "create Task". Click hear.

After the task is created, it is displayed in the backlog and on the board in the "To do" area.

To delete a task, you can simply press the X in the backlog and it will be deleted.

To change the status, simply drag and drop the task into the desired area.

You can change everything in the board by clicking on the task. Don't forget to press the "Save" button.
